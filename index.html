Our School

A Legacy of Excellence in Bikaner

Established in 2019, Decent Global School has rapidly emerged as a beacon of quality education in Bikaner, Rajasthan. We follow a modern curriculum designed to meet global standards while staying rooted in our cultural values.

Our philosophy revolves around the belief that every child is unique. We focus on academic rigor balanced with character development, ensuring that our students are not just exam-ready, but life-ready. Located in a peaceful civil area, our campus provides the perfect environment for focused learning.
